What file do I upload for attendance?

Upload the Sentral absence_list export on the Admin page. The app accepts .xls, .xlsx, and .csv.

Do I need to pick the term or week before uploading?

No. The backend reads the dates inside the uploaded report and infers the correct year, term, and week automatically.

How is a missed roll call counted?

A row counts when the absence is unexplained and the time indicates the student missed morning roll call. Unexplained full-day absences also count as missed roll call.

Why might a student still get a good status if they are not in the report?

The roster is treated as the full student list. If a rostered student does not appear in the uploaded absence report for that week, the app treats them as present and on time.

What do the statuses mean?

goat means on time 4 out of 4 days, sad1 means 3 out of 4, sad2 means 2 out of 4, sad3 means 1 out of 4, and sad4 means 0 out of 4.

What should I upload first?

Upload the roster first, then upload attendance reports. The roster provides the roll classes, aliases, and student identities used during attendance processing.
